How ACD affects your ACO: ACD is a pre-negotiated discount that reduces the customer's invoice. Because ACO credits are applied to the net invoice (after ACD), milestone attainment and the max ACO award are both calculated on post-ACD spend. A higher ACD reduces the net incremental spend, which in turn reduces the Max ACO Award.
